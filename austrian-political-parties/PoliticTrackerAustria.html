Politic Tracker Austria

1. Democracy Austria - Other Countries

1.1 We will bring you an insight into the origin and evolution
of Austrian politics and the Austrian version of democracy.

1.2 In order to to make Austrian democracy more easily to understand you will
be able to compare Austrians democracy to democratic countries around the globe.

2. The Austrian Political System

2.1 This section will briefly explain how and what the Austrian
political system is built on, who holds power, responsibilities and tasks
in that system and how it evolved since it´s establishment in 1920.

2.2 This section will inform you about the current government, who they
consist of, what they have promised, done yet and what they are working on.

3. The Austrian Political Parties

3.1 We will offer detailed profiles of political parties and high-profile politicians
including party and carrer history, along with political focus, intentions,
views on hot-topics, current project and public approval over the years
to inform you sufficiently so you can form your opinion based on facts and not sayings.

3.2 You will be able to compare party profiles side by side to ensure
to stay on point while informing yourself about the political parties.

3.3 Recent polls and surveys will keep you updated what the public
what the public thinks about the current government and their undertakings.

3.4 We will also keep you updated about all of the always present
controversies and stories sorrounding Austria´s politicians.

3.5 Finally we will bring you a minigame in form of a web application
in which you can brawl over the position of reigning chancellor
as top candidates like Kurz, Rendi-Wagner or Hofer.
