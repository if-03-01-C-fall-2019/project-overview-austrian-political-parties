Separation of powers

Political power in Austria is separated between 3 branches:

  1. The executive branch responsible for governing and exercising authority in Austria
  2. The legislative branch responsible for passing new laws on federal and national level
  3. The judicial branch responsible for interpretation of and judgement based on the laws
